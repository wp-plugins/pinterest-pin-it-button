This plugin simply places a Pinterest "Pin It" Button to the bottom or top of your posts.

== Description ==

Encourage your users to pin your posts and images on Pinterest with this easy-to-use "Pin It" button plugin.

This plugin is currently under development. Get updates and more info at:

[pinterestplugin.com](http://pinterestplugin.com)
